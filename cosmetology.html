Cosmetology

Description

Cosmetology is the study and application of beauty treatments, such as hairstyling, skincare, cosmetics, manicures and pedicures, and hair removal. Cosmetologists can work in salons or spas, and may specialize in one or more areas. Some cosmetology specialties include:



  • Hair: Hairstyling, hair coloring, hair treatments, and advanced haircutting
  • Skin: Skincare basics, advanced skincare, and cosmetic and relaxing skin treatments
  • Nails: Nail care basics, advanced nail techniques, and nail artistry
  • Makeup: Makeup basics, bridal makeup, and beauty-enhancement services
