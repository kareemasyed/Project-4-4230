Makeup Artistry

Description

Makeup artistry is the art of applying makeup and prosthetics to people for various productions, such as film, television, theater, fashion, and magazines. Makeup artists, also known as MUAs, need to have a variety of skills, including: color theory, application techniques, adaptability to different skin types, and client communication.



  • Makeup artists need a variety of skills to succeed in their careers, including hard and soft skills
  • Technical skills: Knowledge of makeup theory and application, and how to apply products in a specific order to achieve a desired look
  • Soft skills: Creativity, interpersonal skills, adaptability, and the ability to get along with clients and directors
  • Other skills: Attention to detail, time management, business acumen, hygiene practices, and a commitment to continuous learning
