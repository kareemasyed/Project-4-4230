Analogue Circuits

Description

This course develops a basic understanding of the fundamentals and principles of analog circuits and electronic devices in electrical and electronic engineering. This understanding is a critical step towards being able to design new electronic circuits or use them appropriately as part of a larger engineering system.



  • Non-linear circuit analysis, diodes, load line concepts, introduction to the MOSFET
  • Source degenerated common source amplifier, cascode and cascaded circuits
  • Differential amplifiers, common mode and differential mode gains, CMRR, structure of a complete amplifier
  • Poles and zeros of cascode amplifier, Miller theorem, phase margin, unity gain bandwidth, compensation of the cascaded amplifier
