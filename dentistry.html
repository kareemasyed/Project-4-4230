Dentistry

Description

Dentistry, also known as dental medicine or oral medicine, is a branch of medicine that focuses on the mouth, gums, and teeth. It involves the study, prevention, diagnosis, management, and treatment of conditions, disorders, and diseases of the mouth. Dentistry is primarily concerned with maintaining oral health, but it also has an aesthetic focus.

Why be a dentist?



Dentistry is largely based on maintaining proper oral health, but is also an aesthetically... Some types of dentistry include:

  • Restorative dentistry: Focuses on repairing or replacing teeth to improve chewing function and oral health.
  • General dentists, also known as family dentists, perform restorative dentistry, which can include procedures like crowns, bridges, implants, and dentures.
  • Cosmetic dentistry: Focuses on improving the appearance of a smile.
  • Some dental procedures offer both functional and cosmetic improvements (aesthetic restorative dentistry).
