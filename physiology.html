Human Physiology

Description

Human physiology is the study of how the human body functions in health and disease. It examines the physical, mechanical, and biochemical functions of the body, including how molecules behave in cells and how organ systems work together. Physiology also helps us understand how the body adapts to physical activity, stress, and disease.

What Physiology Tells Us About the Body???



  • The cardiovascular system -- your heart and blood vessels
  • The digestive system -- the stomach, intestines, and other organs that digest food
  • The endocrine system -- glands that make hormones, the chemicals that control many body functions
  • The immune system -- your body's defense against germs and disease
