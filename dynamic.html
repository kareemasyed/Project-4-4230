Description

  • ME 240 Dynamics and Control of Mechanical Systems: Students learn to derive equations of motion of rigid multi-body mechanical systems, solve them using Matlab, and model and analyze feedback controlled dynamical systems.
  • Dynamical System and Control: It covers how dynamical systems can be expressed as differential equations and how mathematical models can provide insight into the behavior of real-life systems.
  • Dynamics and Control of Mechanical Systems: Unlock the power to shape and control motion in the world around us. Dynamics & Controls studies mechanical systems to make them perform in a specific way. Imagine you're driving a car and you want it to maintain a certain speed or stay in a lane automatically.
