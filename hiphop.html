Hip-Hop

Description

Hip hop dance is a style of movement that combines freestyle movements with music and is often performed to hip hop music.

Check out the video below to know more..



  • Hip hop dance is part of a larger culture that includes other elements like DJing, graffiti, emceeing, and breaking
  • DJs, also known as turntablists, create hip hop music in real time by isolating and looping breakbeats from songs using record players or electronic devices.
  • Some of the main styles of hip hop dance include: Popping, Locking, Breaking, Jerking, and Krumping.
  • Hip hop dance can be a full-body workout with both high-impact and anaerobic health benefits. It can raise your heart rate to improve cardiovascular health, and strengthen your arms, legs, and core muscles to improve joint and bone health.
